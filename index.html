01 — About

I turn spreadsheets into arguments worth acting on

I'm a Junior Data Analyst based in Baku, finishing my Computer Science degree at Azerbaijan Technical University. I like the unglamorous part of the job most — cleaning a dataset until it stops lying to you — because that's where the real insight usually shows up.
